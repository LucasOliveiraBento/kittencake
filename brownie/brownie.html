Brownie Recipe

Ingredients and their Quantities

✔ 6 tablespoons full of unsalted margarine

✔ 3/4 cup (chocolate tea)

✔ 1/2 cup (tea) powdered chocolate

✔ 1 and 1/4 cup (tea) wheat flour

✔ 2 cups (tea) sugar

✔ 4 eggs

✔ 2 pinches of salt

✔ 1 teaspoons of vanilla extract or essence

✔ 1 tablet of dark chocolate, cut off into cubes

✔ 1/2 cup (tea) of chopped walnuts or granulated cashew nuts

Method of Preparation

1️⃣ Mix eggs and sugar.

2️⃣ Then add all the other ingredients to form a uniform cream.

3️⃣ Pour into a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake in a medium oven for 40 minutes.

4️⃣ The brownie will be ready when the top is lightly colored and, when you stick a toothpick, it is slightly damp (due to the melted chocolate).

5️⃣ Cut into squares while still hot and serve with a scoop of ice cream, or freeze in a freezer bag.

6️⃣ To thaw, place the brownie on a dessert plate and heat it in the microwave on high power for 1 minute.
