Cupcake Recipe

Ingredients and their Quantities

✔ 2 tablespoons (30 ml) of oil

✔ 1 carrot

✔ 2 eggs

✔ 1 cup (tea) of sugar

✔ 1 cup and 1/4 (150 g) of Wheat Flour

✔ 1 teaspoon of powdered yeast

To the Cover:

✔ 3 tablespoons (100 g) of milk cream

✔ 1/4 box (50 g) of heavy cream

✔ 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder

Method of Preparation for Dough

1️⃣ Put the blender carrot, oil, eggs, sugar in a blender and beat very well.

2️⃣ In a bowl, mix the flour and baking powder. Pour the liquid mixture into a bowl and gradually add the dry mixture, mixing with a fouet.

3️⃣ Then put it in the cupcake pans. Bake in an oven heated to 180°C for approximately 15 minutes.

Preparation for Cover

1️⃣ For the frosting, mix the milk cream, the cream and the cocoa. Cover the cupcakes.
