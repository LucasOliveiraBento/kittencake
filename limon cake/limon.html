Limon Cake Recipe

Ingredients and their Quantities

✔ 2 eggs (white in snow)

✔ 1 and 1/2 cup of sugar

✔ 3 tablespoons of margarine

✔ juice of 1 or 2 lemons (depends on size)

✔ lemon zest

✔ 3 and ¹ / 2 cups of wheat flour

✔ 1 cup of milk

✔ 2 tablespoons of baking powder

To the Cover:

✔ 2/3 can of condensed milk

✔ zest of 1 small lemon

✔ 1/2 lemon juice

Method of Preparation for Dough

1️⃣ Beat the yolks, sugar and margarine in a blender.

2️⃣ Then add the juice and lemon zest, milk and flour little by little.

3️⃣ Finally, gently mix the egg whites and the yeast.

4️⃣ Spread the dough on a greased medium rectangular baking sheet and bake in the oven (already preheated) for about 40 minutes at a temperature of 180ºC.

Preparation for Cover

1️⃣ Mix everything in a small saucepan and bring to the fire, stirring constantly until it boils.
