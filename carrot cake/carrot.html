Carrot Cake

Ingredients and their Quantities

✔ 2 coarsely peeled and chopped carrots

✔ 1 cup (tea) of (a little more to grease the pan)

✔ 3 eggs

✔ 1½ cup (tea) of sugar

✔ 2 cups (tea) of wheat flour (a little more to flour the pan)

✔ 1 tablespoon of powdered yeast

To the Cover:

✔ 3 tablespoons of margarine

✔ 3 tablespoons of sugar

✔ 2 tablespoons of soluble chocolate powder

✔ ½x tea (tea) of milk

Method of Preparation for Dough

1️⃣ In a blender, beat carrots, margarine and eggs;

2️⃣ In a container, place the cream of carrots and add the sifted sugar and flour and mix well;

3️⃣ Add the yeast and mix a little more;

4️⃣ Pour the dough into a pan with a hole in the middle, greased and floured, and bake in a medium oven for approximately 45 minutes

Preparation for Cover

1️⃣ In a saucepan, place all the icing ingredients and cook, mixing continuously, until it forms a smooth icing;

2️⃣ Unmold the cake and cover with the icing. Serve.
